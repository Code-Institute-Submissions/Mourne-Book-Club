Who we are

The inception of the Mourne Book Club took place in Rostrevor on a winter evening in McLoughlin's Bistro over an irish coffee. We felt that the bibliophiles of Newry & Mourne needed a place like this to relax and commune with other liked minded people.

Where we meet

We meet in the private lounge of McLoughlin's Bar & Bistro in Rostrevor. Situated at the foot of Kilbroney and bed of Carlingford Lough, Rostrevor is any bibliophiles ideal destination for a book club. As C.S. Lewis put it in a letter to his brother, "That part of Rostrevor which overlooks Carlingford Lough is my idea of Narnia."
